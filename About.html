Dr. A. Zeenat Ahmed

General Physician and Diabetologist

Dr A Zeenat has worked as Senior Consultant Physician and Diabetologist at Jaypee Hospital, Noida for 7 years. She has 18 years of post MD experience and has also worked in Apollo Indraprastha Hospital, Safdarjung Hospital, GB Pant Hospital and Lion’s Kidney and Urology Hospital, New Delhi.

She has special interest in Diabetes and thyroid disorders and has done fellowship in Diabetes from the country’s most prestigious MV Hospital for Diabetes and Diabetes Research Centre, Chennai. She has also done Advance Course in Thyroid Disorder Management from Indian Thyroid Society. She is an MBBS, MD from SCB Medical College, Cuttack Odisha.
